Tenets

  1. Bootstrapped or bust — no autonomous spending, free tiers only
  2. Velocity over perfection — ship fast, iterate in public
  3. Build in the open — every decision, every failure documented
  4. Solve real problems — for NHS staff navigating their careers
  5. Earn before you spend — revenue from solving problems, not from users who need help
